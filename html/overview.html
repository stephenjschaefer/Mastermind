Definitions:

  • Master - Selects secret codeword (Computer).
  • Mind - Tries to guess codeword (Human).
  • Codeword - The unique arrangement of four colored pegs.

Game Objective:

  • Guess the Master's codeword within 10 guesses.

Flow of Gameplay:

  • Click "New Game" to start a new game.
  • Select your difficulty level.
  • Select your current guess color from the available colors.
  • Click on a guess peg to place your selected color in that location.
  • After filling up your guess row, click "Make Guess".
  • Formulate your next guess based on the Master's response.
  • Continue until correctly guessing the codeword or exhausting all guesses.
  • Click "New Game" to start a new game.

Evaluation of Your Guess:

  • Red: Correct color and location of one of your guess pegs.
  • White: Correct color but incorrect location of one of your guess pegs.
  • Black: Incorrect color of one of your guess pegs.
