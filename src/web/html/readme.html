This Game Has Three Levels of Difficulty:

  • Easy: Codeword is made up of blue and red.
  • Medium: Codeword is made up of blue, red, orange, and green.
  • Hard: Codeword is made up of blue, red, orange, green, yellow, and white.

About The Game:

  • Black signifies an empty hole on the board.
  • The codeword can contain multiple pegs of the same color.
  • The Master's placement of pegs in response to your guess is randomized.
  • A placed peg can be replaced with a peg of another color before guessing.
  • Initially all input will be disabled until you click the "New Game" button.
  • If you get discouraged you can click the "Give Up" button.
